About Us

Andy has been successfully teaching learner drivers for many years and is a top-grade instructor (Grade A). He is a DVSA approved driving instructor (ADI) and has been DBS checked. He is a very patient, friendly, and understanding instructor helping to make your learning experience more enjoyable. We find that learning in this type of environment will generally speed up the time required to reach the required standards.

Tuition Vehicle

Lessons are carried out in the latest model of the Vauxhall Corsa 1.2 Sri Turbo. Includes dual controls, air conditioning, heated seats, and steering wheel, and many other features to help make the learning experience more enjoyable.
